Assignment #1

Needfinding

This assignment was the first of our project in which we interview random strangers or someone we knew in the field of education. This assignment helped us to gain some insight into people beliefs and understanding of technology and specifically its use in the classroom. From the user stories, we developed our own beliefs and understandings, with the goal of building a solution in the future.
