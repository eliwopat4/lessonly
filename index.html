Overview

We are a group of Stanford students, all interested in the Education System and specifically where technology is used in the classroom. Together our group forms Helm (Hannes, Eli, Lee, and Mia). Our shared passion for education and how technology should be better designed and implemented for the class room motivated us to create Lessonly- the online teachers' lounge where teaching resources can be shared and gain valuable feedback.
